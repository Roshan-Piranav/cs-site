## How to Update

1. Open the original Google Doc.
2. Launch the extension.
3. Choose the post you want to update from your site.
4. **Update Your Post**.
5. Click **Publish to WP**.

## Sync Options

- Replace full content
- Only update specific blocks

> Warning: Updating will overwrite the post content on your site.
