## Steps to Create a Post

1. Open a Google Doc with your content.
2. Open the extension sidebar.
3. Choose a connected WordPress site.

4. Select **Create New Post or New Page**
5. Customize post settings:

   - Title
   - Slug
   - Category
   - Tags
   - Status (Draft, Published)

6. Click **Publish**
7. Once the content is successfully pushed to the WordPress site, a link will be generated below. You can click on it to view the published content immediately.

> **Tip:** You can preview the post before final publishing.
